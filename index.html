Movie Collection

When They Cry: Umineko

A mystery movie that not only will test your wits, but also take you to a rollercoaster of emotions as you delve into the depth of mystery set in Rokkenjima Island. Combining the element of mystery and fantasy, it's simply a masterpiece of its genre.

Excellent /⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

The description tells no lies. I was at the edge of my seat the whole time when watching it. Definitely a movie of the year. Heck, even a decade! A cruel move to other mystery movies for setting the new standard way too high.

The Song of Saya

A dark romance movie that will blow your expectation regarding its core of genre. Unfolding a tale of love in the world of psychological horror, The Song of Saya is well-known for its gruesome approach to the dark side of love, unfolding a new perspective on how you view the very world itself.

Good /⭐⭐⭐⭐

Definitely one enjoyable movie. It kept me wondering about the next possible things that could happen, which always turned out to be outside your expectation. I would strongly advice you to watch it alone though, as some scenes could be seen as inappropriate. This movie offers a lot of immoral value, while at the same time making you encourage that same aspect of immorality.

Clannad

Drama, family, responsibility. This movie involves many aspect of life that will make you realize the importance in what seems to be mundanity. Introducing you to the bright, light-hearted side of life in the beginning, this movie will then wreck your heart by taking those same happy moments to a painful reality that the characters will have to face.

Bad /⭐⭐

Haven't finished it yet due to how unneccessary long the slice of life part felt. People kept telling me what a great movie this is, but I just couldn't endure those boring mundane everyday scenes at the first half of this movie. For god's sake, I have watched a thriller movie with better slice of life scenes! Maybe this one's not really my cup of tea, but I promise myself to come back to this movie again someday and give it a second try. But as of now, I just don't care anymore.......

Perfect Blue

The truest sense of a psychological thriller movie. This movie shows elaborately just how much a perspective could change the very flow of reality itself. It delves deep into the character's sanity, identity, and safety.

Excellent /⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

If anyone's going to ask me for the best thriller movie ever, my answer would forever be this! I love psychology, I love thriller, I love scenes that will confuse the shit out of me, which will turn out to be mindblowing plot-twists that will change the entire flow of the story. This comment section won't be enough to elaborate how much love I got for this masterpiece of work. Perfect Blue is just...perfect. An absolute classic that will prove quite a tough challenge for other thriller movies to take its throne.
